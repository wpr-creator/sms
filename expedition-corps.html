✅ NEEDS

A need is something people must have to stay healthy, safe, or ready for school. Needs usually come first when people make spending choices.

  • Water
  • Apple
  • Sandwich
  • Chips
  • Doctor visit

⭐ WANTS

A want is something people would like to have. Wants can be fun, useful, or popular, but people can live without them.

  • NeeDoh
  • Labubu
  • Stanley cup
  • PS5
  • Extra toy

🛍️ GOODS

A good is something you can touch, carry, eat, wear, or use. Goods are objects people buy.

  • Apple
  • Water
  • Sandwich
  • NeeDoh
  • Stanley cup

🤝 SERVICES

A service is work someone does for another person. You pay for the action or help, not for an object you carry away.

  • Haircut
  • Dentist visit
  • Doctor visit
  • Bus ride
